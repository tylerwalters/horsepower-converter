Horsepower Converter

What power would you like?

As I was driving one day, I considered the term horsepower. It is an interesting way to measure the power of an engine. Then I thought it would be even more interesting to measure the power of my engine in terms of a Tyrannosaurus Rex. And so the Horsepower Converter was born.

Where horsepower for a specific conversion was not available I used a trap speed formula (weight * (velocity/234)^3) to estimate using weight and speed. For example, the Tyrannosaurus Rex has a weight of 17990 lbs and had a speed of 18 mile per hour (both estimated obviously) which gives us 8.1884387801548 horsepower.
